ABOUT US

CppAfrica is Africa's community of C++ developers seeeking to promote the learning and use of C++ through research and collaborations. we are currently growing and seek to expand throughout all the 54 countries in Africa. we are currently present in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and a few more to mention. to serve as a larger group and also contribute as a worldwide center for C++ development, we hope to commence an annual C++ conference where we would invite the worldwide C++ communities to Africa to collaborate and learn on the advances the world is making towards the learning of C++ in a very welcoming environment in one of the largest continent in the world. Inclusivity and diversity is our driving force..
